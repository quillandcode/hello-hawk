>> System_Expectation: Integrity Design

Dishonesty is often a "survival mechanism" for an overloaded student. Let's shift the conversation from judgment to design. Parents, please consider these 5 diagnostic questions:

  1. "How can I support you when school feels overwhelming?" (Be a resource, not a scoreboard.)
  2. "What pressures do you feel from friends?" (Expose the social normalization of shortcuts.)
  3. "What matters more—grades or actually learning?" (Separate self-worth from the number.)
  4. "When are you most tempted to take shortcuts?" (Identify the failure patterns: deadlines vs. difficulty.)
  5. "What would make it easier to stay honest?" (Remove the conditions that make cheating feel necessary.)
