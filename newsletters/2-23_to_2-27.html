>> Academic Integrity: Growth in the Right Direction

I am seeing many students use AI appropriately—for studying, reviewing, and understanding the "why" behind concepts instead of just asking for answers.

Even so, I still run into cases where I cannot fully hear a student's individual voice in classwork and homework responses. This does not automatically mean dishonesty, but it does raise concerns and prompts closer review.

Please continue encouraging students to use AI as a learning tool, not as a replacement for their own thinking.
