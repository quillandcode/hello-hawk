>> Security_Policy: Academic Integrity

I am resetting expectations around integrity. My goal is not just to prevent cheating, but to prepare students for the industry. Shortcuts taken to earn a grade become a handicap later—they rob students of confidence and professional habits.

Our CS team is actively working to change the culture behind academic dishonesty. We want students to leave prepared for industry-level work, both technically and professionally.
