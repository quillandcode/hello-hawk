AP Computer Science A

A college-level course centered on solving problems through careful planning, algorithms, and code. While students learn Java, the deeper focus is on applying structured problem-solving strategies they already know to increasingly complex challenges.

Honors Computer Science 1

An accelerated introduction to computer science for beginners using Python. Students learn how computers work, explore number systems such as binary and hexadecimal, practice digital citizenship, and begin developing strong, code-based problem-solving skills that prepare them for advanced coursework and certifications.

Advanced Cloud Computing (AWS Academy)

A self-paced, hands-on course built around AWS Academy curriculum and supported by guided instruction. Students earn foundational cloud certification knowledge and apply what they learn through realistic, scenario-based assessments, solving problems for fictitious business clients and preparing for additional AWS and AI certifications.
